About Us

"At JR INDUSTRIES, we are dedicated to redefining architectural spaces through precision engineering and artistic metalwork. Based in Bengaluru, we have established ourselves as a premier manufacturer specializing in bespoke steel staircases, high-performance fabrications, and luxury brass railings. Our craftsmanship combines the industrial strength of modern steel with the timeless elegance of polished brass, ensuring that every project—from intricate residential handrails to complex commercial structures—is both a functional necessity and a visual centerpiece. Driven by a commitment to quality and safety, JR INDUSTRIES utilizes advanced machinery and skilled artistry to deliver durable, custom-tailored solutions that stand the test of time. We don't just build structures; we forge lasting impressions."
